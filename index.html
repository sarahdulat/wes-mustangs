About Wesley

Wesley has been restoring Mustangs for over 15 years. He only uses 100% original parts, and in the rare case that a part hasn't made it 45 years, only the finest old new stock (ONS) or great reproductions will do. From frame to paint job the integrity of the Ford Mustang is mainted to the highest standard.
